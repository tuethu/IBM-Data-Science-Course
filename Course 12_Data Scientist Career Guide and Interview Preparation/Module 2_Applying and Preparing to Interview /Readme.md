- What is an informational interview? 
An interview someone holds with a professional to learn more about a field of work.

- What is the top networking website? 
LinkedIn

- Which of the following is a “red flag” in a job listing, indicating that you should consider very carefully before applying? <br/>
Unrealistic requirements 
Companies that demand difficult requirements, such as several years’ experience for an entry-level role, may not understand the role or may be exploitative. 

- What are the three basic components of a good elevator pitch? <br/>
Introduce yourself, tell what you have done recently, and list your professional goals 

- When you are reading a company’s website because you plan to interview with them, why should you pay attention to keywords you spot on the site? <br/>
It can help you understand the image the company wants to project and maintain. 

- A company website is a good place to research a company you’re interested in. Why should you pay attention to the language used in the website text? <br/>
Repeated keywords can give you an idea of what is important to the company. 

- Why should you check social media to find out about a company you want to join?<br/> 
You can understand the company’s image and corporate culture from what they post. 

- Which three of the following are effective networking methods?<br/> 
	- Talk to friends about your job search. 
	- Take classes to brush up on your professional skills. 
	- Join professional groups and attend events. 

- What percentage of recruiters use LinkedIn as part of their candidate search?<br/> 
Almost 90% 

- Which of the following is true about working as a contractor and full-time employee (FTE)?<br/> 
Both contractors and FTEs usually work full-time hours. 

- What is a “red flag” that might indicate thinking twice about responding to a job listing?<br/> 
Broad expectations about the position with few specifics 

- Which two statements are true about qualifications in job listings?<br/> 
	- You don’t need to have all of the qualifications listed. 
	- The qualifications may include both hard and soft skills. 

- What is true about rehearsing for interviews?<br/> 
It’s important to practice articulating what you want to say. 

- What is a mock interview?<br/> 
A practice interview that enables you to rehearse your answers at home 

- What is a good way to prepare for first interviews?<br/> 
Use your notes to compose questions you want to ask the company.
